Resources

Below are a selection of resources that will assist you in learning more about Roller Derby and even start playing the sport! There are a wide number of teams worldwide, both women's and mens teams along with opportunities to not only play the sport but also to coach or officate.

WFTDA.com
The main website for the WFTDA: Womens Flat Track Derby Association.
The hosts a wealth of information on the sport Roller Derby.
WFTDA Live Rankings
 WFTDA live world wide rankings for Womens Roller Derby
Mens Roller Derby Association
 Mens Roller Derby Association Website
MRDA Rankings
 Mens Roller Derby Association Worldwide Rankings
Dublin Roller Derby Learn to skate: send an email to enroll
Learn to Skate with Dublin Roller Derby. Dublin Roller Derby run an intorduction to the very basics of learning to skate.

After attending this course and suitably mastering the very basics of skating,
you can advance on how to learn more advanced skills that will allow you to start playing the sport!
DublinRollerDerby.com
Dublin Roller Derby are Irelands oldest and largest Roller Derby teams.

Established in 2009.
Join Dublin Roller Derby
Details on this page on how to join Dublin Roller Derby. You can not only join as a player, but can also train to become a Referee or a Non Skating Offical (NSO).

Many players will also offciate games to help their league or when they retire from playing.
